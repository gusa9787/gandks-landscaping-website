Our Services

At G&K's Landscaping & Construction, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch landscaping and construction services tailored to meet the unique needs of both residential and commercial clients. From creating breathtaking landscapes to efficient snow removal, our team is committed to enhancing the beauty and functionality of your property.

Lawn Care

We offer comprehensive solutions, including lawn mowing, flower maintenance, and sod installation. Our expert team meticulously mows lawns for a neat and polished look, nurtures flowers to enhance their beauty, and installs high-quality sod to create lush, green lawns.

Landscape Design and Installation

We provide personalized consultations to craft the perfect landscaping design tailored to your preferences and property needs. Our experts assist with plant selection, ensuring the right choices for your environment.

Finally, we bring the design to life with meticulous installation, creating a beautiful, cohesive outdoor space that enhances your property’s aesthetics and value.

Hardscape Construction

We specialize in installing patios, landscaped walkways, sitting walls, retaining walls, fire pits, and landscape lighting. Our skilled team ensures each hardscape element is crafted with precision and durability, creating functional and aesthetically pleasing outdoor spaces.

Snow and Ice Removal

We provide hands-on shoveling for precise snow removal and use truck and skid plowing for larger areas. To tackle icy conditions, we offer hands-on salting and truck sanding, effectively preventing hazards.

Our dedicated team is committed to keeping your residential or commercial property clear and secure, no matter how harsh the winter weather.

Irrigation and Sprinkler Systems

We install efficient irrigation systems tailored to your property’s needs, covering everything from sprinklers to drip irrigation. Our team also provides expert repair and maintenance to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Additionally, we conduct seasonal adjustments to adapt to changing weather conditions, ensuring your landscape receives the right amount of water year-round.

Water Features

Our skilled team can create anything imaginable, from serene waterfalls to elegant fountains, tailored to your vision and property. Each water feature is designed with precision and artistry, adding a touch of tranquility and sophistication to your landscape.

Outdoor Living Spaces

Our expertise includes designing and building outdoor decks equipped with fully functional outdoor kitchens, perfect for entertaining and enjoying nature. Whether you envision a cozy retreat for family gatherings or a sophisticated space for hosting guests, our custom outdoor living solutions are crafted to meet your needs and enhance your lifestyle.

Fence and Concrete Installation

Our skilled team installs a variety of fence styles and materials, tailored to meet your specific needs and preferences. Whether you’re looking for a decorative boundary, a secure enclosure, or added privacy, our high-quality fences are designed to complement your landscape while providing durability and functionality.

In addition, we offer expert concrete installation services for driveways, walkways, patios, and more, ensuring long-lasting and aesthetically pleasing results that enhance your property's overall appeal.
